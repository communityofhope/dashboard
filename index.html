About the MAPS Framework
COH measures discipleship health through four stages of engagement: Disinterested → reaching those outside the church, Interested → engaging new visitors, Growing Together → developing committed disciples, Fully Devoted → equipping core leaders.

📋 Stage totals and annual figures sourced from 2025 Annual Report. Quarterly figures from internal metrics sheet. 2026 reporting uses Trimester system: T1 (Jan–May) · T2 (Jun–Aug) · T3 (Sep–Dec).
